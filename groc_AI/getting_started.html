Welcome to the McNabb-McNeil Family Tree Research Project

This page helps you explore our family history using AI with Grok. Follow these simple steps to get started, even if you’re new to technology!

Step 1: Learn How to Copy and Paste

If you don’t know how to copy and paste on your device (phone, tablet, or laptop), don’t worry! Go to the Grok prompt and ask:

“How do I copy and paste on my phone/tablet/laptop?”

Grok will guide you based on your device. Once you’ve learned how to copy and paste, come back here to continue.

Step 2: Start Your Research with the Setup Program

The Setup Program provides instructions and family tree data to start your research session with Grok.

Click the button below to open the Setup Program, then copy the entire text and paste it into your Grok session to begin exploring the family tree.

Open Setup Program

Step 3: Explore More with OCR Text Pages

For additional research, you can use scanned pages from our family history documents. These pages contain text you can search and share with Grok. Expanded sections are included in the copy buffer, and searching automatically expands matching sections.

Click the button below to open the OCR Text Pages, select pages of interest, copy the text, and paste it into your Grok session for deeper insights. Keep an eye on the buffer size (shown on the page) and try to keep it under 100,000 characters to ensure smooth copying.

Open OCR Text Pages

Need help? Just ask Grok for assistance at any time!
