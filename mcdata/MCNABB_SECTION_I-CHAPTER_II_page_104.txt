THIS IS AN ACCOUNT OF THE NAMING OF ELIZABETHTON,
TENN, IN 1796, WHEN TENN BECAME A STATE. SUCH
NAMING WAS IN HONOUR OF A VIRGINIA GIRL, ELIZABETH
TAYLOR, WHOSE HUSBAND, DAVID MCNABB OF AUGUSTA
COUNTY, VIRGINIA, WAS A REVOLUTIONARY WAR OFFICER
IN NORTH CAROLINA.
ELIZABETH TAYLOR MCNABB SHARED THIS HONOUR WITH
ELIZABETH MCLIN CARTER.
